The Sanctuary — God's Blueprint

"Let them make me a sanctuary; that I may dwell among them." — Exodus 25:8

The Structure

CourtyardAltar of burnt offering, Laver
Holy PlaceLampstand, Table of showbread, Incense altar
Most Holy PlaceArk of Covenant (Ten Commandments), Mercy seat

Built "according to the pattern" on the mount (Ex. 25:9; Heb. 8:5)

Two Services

DAILY (Tamid): Morning & evening lamb. Individual sin offerings — sin transferred to sanctuary (Lev. 4). Incense morning/evening.

YEARLY (Day of Atonement): High priest enters Most Holy Place. Sanctuary cleansed from accumulated sins (Lev. 16). A day of judgment.

Daniel 8:13 asks a sanctuary question. Daniel 8:14 gives a sanctuary answer. This is a sanctuary prophecy from beginning to end.

Part 1: The Vision of Daniel 8

The Ram — Medo-Persia

Two horns; the higher coming up last. "The ram... are the kings of Media and Persia." (Dan. 8:20)

The He-Goat — Greece

Notable horn; strikes the ram. Great horn breaks; four arise. "The rough goat is the king of Grecia." (Dan. 8:21)

The Little Horn — Rome (Pagan & Papal)

Grows "exceeding great." Removes the daily (tamid) — assault on Christ's heavenly priesthood. Pagan Rome: Crucified Christ, destroyed the temple (A.D. 70). Papal Rome: Substituted earthly priests for the one heavenly Priest. (EGW: Christ in His Sanctuary, p. 61)

Part 2: The Question & the Answer

"How long shall be the vision concerning the daily sacrifice, and the transgression of desolation, to give both the sanctuary and the host to be trodden under foot? And he said unto me, Unto two thousand and three hundred days; then shall the sanctuary be cleansed." Daniel 8:13-14

The Question (v. 13)

Every word is sanctuary language: "daily" (tamid), "sanctuary," "trodden under foot."

The Answer (v. 14)

2,300 days — Hebrew tsadaq: "vindicated," "restored." LXX: katharisthesetai (same root as Heb. 9:23).

Year-Day Principle (Num. 14:34; Eze. 4:6)
2,300 prophetic days = 2,300 literal years

Part 3: The Connection to Daniel 9

Gabriel Returns

Daniel 8 ends with the 2,300 days unexplained. In Daniel 9, Gabriel returns: "I am now come forth to give thee skill and understanding... consider the vision." (Dan. 9:22-23)

70 Weeks "Cut Off"

Hebrew chatak = "cut off" from the 2,300 years. 490 years form the first portion. "Anoint the most Holy" = inaugurate the heavenly sanctuary (Heb. 9:11-12, 24).

The Starting Point: 457 B.C.

The decree of Artaxerxes I (Ezra 7:12-26) to restore and build Jerusalem. Confirmed by Ptolemy's Canon, the Elephantine Papyri, and Greek Olympiad records.

Parallels the Daily Service (Tamid)

  • Advocacy — 1 John 2:1
  • Mediation — 1 Tim. 2:5

Evidence: Rev. 1:12-13 (lampstands); Rev. 8:3-4 (incense)

Parallels the Day of Atonement

Part 5: The Terminus — A.D. 1844

457 B.C. + 2,300 years = A.D. 1844 "Then shall the sanctuary be cleansed"

What Sanctuary?

Earthly temple destroyed A.D. 70. The sanctuary cleansed in 1844 must be the heavenly sanctuary.

Christ moved from Holy Place (forgiveness) to Most Holy Place (judgment/blotting out).

Oct. 22, 1844 = 10 Tishri

Day of Atonement fell on October 22 (Karaite reckoning). Rev. 11:19 — Ark seen in heaven. Rev. 3:7-8 — "an open door."

If the spring feasts and 490 years were fulfilled with pinpoint accuracy, we can trust the fall feast fulfillment in 1844.

Part 6: The Judgment Scene — Daniel 7

"I beheld till the thrones were cast down, and the Ancient of days did sit... the judgment was set, and the books were opened." Daniel 7:9-10, 13-14

Key Points

  • A pre-Advent judgment — before Christ's return
  • Son of Man comes to the Ancient of Days — not to earth
  • Books are opened — investigation of records
  • Result: "dominion, and glory, and a kingdom"

This IS the Most Holy Place

  • "Ancient of days did sit" → mercy seat
  • "Thousand thousands" → cherubim
  • "Books were opened" → Ten Commandments — the standard
  • "Son of man came" → Christ entering Most Holy Place

Daniel 7 and Daniel 8:14 describe the same event from different perspectives.

Part 9: Common Objections

"The 2,300 days are literal, not years."

Literal days cannot span Medo-Persia, Greece, and Rome. Year-day principle established (Num. 14:34; Eze. 4:6).

"This is about Antiochus Epiphanes."

Minor king (~3 years), not "exceeding great" (2,300 years). "Prince of the host" = Christ.

"Daniel 9 is not connected to Daniel 8."

Hebrew chatak = "cut off." Gabriel references the prior unexplained vision.

"There is no heavenly sanctuary."

Hebrews 8-9; Revelation depicts altar (8:3-4), lampstands (1:12), Ark (11:19).
